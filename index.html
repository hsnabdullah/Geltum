Golteum Solves Four Key Problems

Affordability

The platform makes gold investing accessible to mass market consumers while lowering entry barriers, unleashing trillions in standard value, and paving the way for a more equal and sustainable society.

Credit Accessibility

Because our fractional NFTs are backed by genuine gold, they will unlock additional credit opportunities, such as borrowing that may not have once been available to certain customers in some parts of the world.

Transparency

On-chain monitoring guarantees full backing with physical gold at all times. Information about each gold bar is stored inside the metadata of Golteum NFTs, which can be redeemed for their physical gold equivalent.

Flexibility

Fractionalized assets on chain allow users to gain access to formerly inaccessible markets. Over time, our platform will become more decentralized, allowing for greater community engagement and a real feeling of ownership.
